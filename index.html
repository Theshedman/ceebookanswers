Login

There are three different login sections on this page: Admin, Candidate and Guest. Those we consider candidates are the early subscribers whose details are registered in our system. If you are a candidate, you should login using the candidate section. Guests are daily subscribers. If you are a guest, use the password that was given you to login under the guest section. Admins should use the admin section to login.

Note: click on the Verify Subscription and enter your phone number to verify your subscription. If you pass the verification, you are a candidate else you are not.
